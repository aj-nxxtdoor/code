About Me

abdifatah Bashir | Photographer

Hello! am abdifatah, a passionate photographer based in the heart of the city. My journey with photography began five years ago with a simple point-and-shoot camera, and it has evolved into a profound love affair with light, composition, and storytelling. I specialize in landscape and portrait photography, always seeking to capture the authentic essence of my subjects. Whether it's the golden hour glow over a mountain range or the genuine smile of a loved one, I believe every moment deserves to be remembered beautifully.
